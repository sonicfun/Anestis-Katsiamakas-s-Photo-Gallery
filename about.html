ANESTIS KATSIAMAKAS

(Larissa-Greece, August 11, 1960 – Athens-Greece, March 18, 2021)

Graduate in Civil Engineering in the National Metsovio Polytechnic. Graduate in Superior Studies in French Institute of Athens and in Photography in FOCUS School. Numerous seminars in Informatics in Greece and abroad. He was awarded the 1st prize of the National Mathematical Society in 1978. He worked, initially, as a software engineer and as a software architect subsequently in major Informatics companies in Greece. The last thirteen years, before he passes away from cancer, he was working in UNISYSTEMS. Photography was a passion he kept as a hobby. The black and white photos in this gallery was part of the collection “WOMEN IN BLACK AND WHITE” presented in FRENCH INSTITUTE OF THESSALONIKI (March 29-April 10, 1993),in HATZIYANNIO MUNICIPAL CULTURAL CENTRE in Larissa (November 20-December 3, 1993) and in the CULTURAL CENTRE OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF ATHENS under the supervision of Artemis Rigopoulou (April 5-15, 1994). Part of the exposition was presented in Niort, France under the supervision of the noted photographer Michel Semeniako in the frame of the international photo competition. The black and white series of photos “NYMPHES” or “BRIDES” was unfinished and wasn’t presented in public. Some of the photos are found and are part of the present gallery. He was awarded the first AGFA Prize in Algarve, Portugal in 1993. In 2020 his photo “ALONE TOGETHER” was among the best 30 in IANOS competition “DIARIES OF QUARANTINE”. He travelled in more than 100 countries taking many pictures. The biggest part of this work hasn’t been found. His photos were published in foreign magazines and newspapers such as the French “CHASSEUR D’ IMAGE”, “LA NOUVELLE REPUBLIQUE”, the Italian “PRIVATE”, the Greek magazines “PHOTOGRAPER”, “PHOTOGRAPHY”, “OPTICON”, “ROCK POWER”, etc and a photo of one of his favorite models the actress Agapi Manoura made a cover of the magazine “PADIOTILEORASSI” (issue 1248).
